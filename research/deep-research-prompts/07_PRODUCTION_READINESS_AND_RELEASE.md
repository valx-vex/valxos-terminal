# Master Prompt: Production Readiness And Release Research

You are researching how to take `VALXOS Terminal` from prototype to production-ready public release.

Research questions:
- What makes a terminal-based developer product feel trustworthy and production ready?
- What testing layers matter most for a product with plugins, hooks, MCP, model providers, and local shell execution?
- How should release channels work: nightly, beta, stable?
- What telemetry, crash reporting, privacy controls, and offline behavior are appropriate?
- What install/update methods matter most across macOS, Linux, and Windows?
- What documentation and onboarding assets drive adoption?
- What security and sandboxing expectations do users have for agentic developer tools?
- Which failure modes most often kill trust in early AI dev tools?

Deliverables:
- Production readiness checklist
- QA strategy
- Compatibility test matrix
- Plugin certification or trust model ideas
- Release strategy
- Observability and privacy recommendations
- Documentation checklist
- Recommended order of hardening work

Output format:
- Markdown
- Include a staged release plan
- Include a `Ship Blockers` section
- Include source links
